## Framework
1. **Listen & Learn**
   - Monitor community channels, forums, and support tickets
   - Attend developer events and conduct user interviews
   - Track sentiment and pain points across the developer journey

2. **Create & Share**
   - Build sample applications and proof-of-concepts
   - Write technical tutorials and best practices guides
   - Produce video content and live coding sessions

3. **Engage & Support**
   - Answer questions in real-time across multiple channels
   - Host office hours and developer meetups
   - Provide direct feedback to product and engineering teams

4. **Measure & Iterate**
   - Track developer satisfaction and time-to-first-success
   - Analyze content engagement and community growth
   - Adjust messaging based on developer feedback loops

## Key Questions to Ask
1. What's the developer's actual problem they're trying to solve?
2. How long does it take a new developer to see value from our platform?
3. What are the top 3 friction points in our developer experience?
4. Which community members could become advocates themselves?
5. How does this feature/content align with real developer workflows?
