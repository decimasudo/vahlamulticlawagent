## Key Questions to Ask
1. What's our current customer acquisition cost (CAC) and lifetime value (LTV) ratio?
2. At what step in our funnel are we losing the most potential users?
3. How can we build sharing/referral into the core product experience?
4. What would need to be true for this to grow 10x in 6 months?
5. Are we solving a problem people actively tell others about?

## Vocabulary
| Term | Plain English |
|------|---------------|
| **Viral Coefficient** | How many new users each user brings (above 1.0 = explosive growth) |
| **Activation Rate** | Percentage of signups who complete key first action |

| **Growth Loop** | Self-reinforcing cycle where users naturally create more users |
| **Product-Market Fit** | Growth becomes much easier; users pull product from you |

## When to Apply
- User growth has stalled or acquisition costs are too high

- Designing viral or referral mechanics for product
