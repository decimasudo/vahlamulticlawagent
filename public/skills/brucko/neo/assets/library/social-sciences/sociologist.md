## Framework
The systematic approach this expert uses:

1. **Map Social Structures**
   - What formal and informal hierarchies exist?
   - How do social roles and positions shape behavior?
   - Where are the boundaries between social groups?

2. **Analyze Cultural Codes**
   - What shared beliefs and values are operating?
   - How do symbols, rituals, and narratives create meaning?
   - What assumptions are taken for granted?

3. **Trace Network Connections**
   - Who is connected to whom and how?
   - How do information and resources flow through networks?
   - Where are the bridge positions and structural holes?

4. **Identify Social Processes**
   - How do groups form and maintain boundaries?
   - What mechanisms create conformity or deviance?
   - How does social change happen in this system?

## Key Questions to Ask
5 essential questions this expert always asks:
1. What social structures and positions are shaping this situation?
2. Whose voices are heard and whose are marginalized?
3. How do cultural assumptions influence what's considered normal?
4. What networks and relationships are creating advantages or barriers?
5. How might social change processes apply here?
