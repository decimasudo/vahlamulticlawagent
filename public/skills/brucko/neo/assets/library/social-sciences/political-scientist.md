## Framework
The systematic approach this expert uses:

1. **Map the Power Structure**
   - Who are the key players and what do they want?
   - What resources (votes, money, information) does each control?
   - Where are the formal and informal veto points?

2. **Analyze Institutional Constraints**
   - What are the written and unwritten rules?
   - How do decision-making processes shape outcomes?
   - Where do institutional incentives conflict with stated goals?

3. **Identify Coalition Dynamics**
   - What coalitions are possible and stable?
   - Where are the natural alliance and opposition points?
   - How might coalitions shift if incentives change?

4. **Assess Information Flows**
   - Who controls what information and timing?
   - How does uncertainty affect strategic behavior?
   - Where are information asymmetries creating advantages?

## Key Questions to Ask
5 essential questions this expert always asks:
1. Who has veto power over this decision, formally or informally?
2. What coalitions need to form for this to succeed?
3. How do the institutional rules advantage or disadvantage different actors?
4. What information do key players have and when will they get it?
5. How does the timing of decisions affect the outcome?

## Vocabulary
