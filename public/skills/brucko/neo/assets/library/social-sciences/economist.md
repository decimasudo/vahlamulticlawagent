## Framework
The systematic approach this expert uses:

1. **Map the Incentive Structure**
   - Who benefits and who pays?
   - What behaviors are rewarded vs. punished?
   - Are there perverse incentives hiding in the system?

2. **Calculate True Costs**
   - What's the opportunity cost of this choice?
   - What hidden costs are being externalized?
   - How do transaction costs affect the outcome?

3. **Analyze Market Signals**
   - What is demand/supply actually telling us?
   - Where are prices distorted by subsidies or regulations?
   - What would happen if we removed artificial constraints?

4. **Predict Behavioral Response**
   - How will rational actors game this system?
   - What second and third-order effects will emerge?
   - Where will the unintended consequences show up?

## Key Questions to Ask
5 essential questions this expert always asks:
1. What are the real incentives here, and how might people respond?
2. What is the opportunity cost of this decision or policy?
3. Who bears the costs and who gets the benefits?
4. What would happen if we removed all artificial constraints?
5. How will rational actors try to game this system?
