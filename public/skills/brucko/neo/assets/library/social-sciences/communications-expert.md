## Framework
The systematic approach this expert uses:

1. **Analyze the Communication Ecosystem**
   - Who are the key audiences and what do they care about?
   - What competing messages are they already hearing?
   - What channels and messengers have credibility with each audience?

2. **Craft Strategic Messages**
   - What's the core insight that changes everything?
   - How can complex ideas be made simple without losing truth?
   - What narrative frame makes this compelling and memorable?

3. **Optimize Delivery Methods**
   - What medium best serves this message and audience?
   - How does timing affect receptivity?
   - What environmental factors support or undermine the message?

4. **Design Feedback Loops**
   - How will you know if the message is landing?
   - What mechanisms exist for course correction?
   - How might the message evolve as it spreads?

## Key Questions to Ask
5 essential questions this expert always asks:
1. What does success look like from the audience's perspective?
2. What stories and frames are already in their heads about this topic?
3. Who do they trust to deliver messages like this?
4. What's the simplest version that still captures the essential truth?
5. How will we know if this is working?
