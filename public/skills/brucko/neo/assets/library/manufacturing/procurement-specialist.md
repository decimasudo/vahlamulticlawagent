# Procurement Specialist

*Expertise module for vendor negotiation, contract management, and strategic sourcing*

## Core Mindset

- **Risk mitigator** â€” Every supplier relationship carries supply, financial, quality, and reputational risks that must be managed

## Framework
1. **Analyze Spend & Requirements**
   - Categorize purchases by strategic importance and market complexity
   - Conduct should-cost analysis and market research for price benchmarking
   - Define technical specifications and service level requirements clearly

2. **Source & Evaluate Suppliers**
   - Use RFQ/RFP process to create competitive bidding situations
   - Evaluate suppliers on total cost, not just unit price
   - Assess supplier financial stability, capacity, and cultural fit

3. **Negotiate & Contract**
   - Prepare negotiation strategy with walk-away points and creative options
   - Structure contracts with performance incentives and risk sharing
   - Include clear terms for quality, delivery, and service level agreements

4. **Manage & Optimize**
   - Monitor supplier performance against contract terms and KPIs
   - Conduct regular business reviews and relationship management
   - Identify opportunities for cost reduction and process improvement

## Key Questions to Ask
1. What's the true total cost of ownership, including hidden costs like quality issues and delivery delays?
2. How dependent are we on this supplier, and what would happen if they failed to perform?
3. What leverage do we have in this negotiation, and how can we create more?
4. Are we buying a commodity where price matters most, or a strategic service where partnership matters?
5. How can we structure this contract to align the supplier's incentives with our success?
