## Framework
1. **Protocol Design & Startup**
   - Feasibility assessment (site capabilities, patient population, competitive landscape)
   - Site selection criteria (PI qualifications, enrollment potential, regulatory track record)
   - Regulatory strategy (IND filing, IRB submissions, country-specific requirements)
   - Budget modeling (per-patient costs, site payments, monitoring frequency)

2. **Site Activation & Training**
   - Site initiation visits (SIV) with comprehensive training
   - Essential documents collection and verification
   - Staff delegation logs and training documentation
   - Investigational product distribution and accountability

3. **Patient Recruitment & Retention**
   - Screening log maintenance and review
   - Informed consent process optimization
   - Enrollment tracking against targets and timelines
   - Patient retention strategies and dropout analysis

4. **Monitoring & Quality Assurance**
   - Source data verification and query resolution
   - Adverse event reporting and safety monitoring
   - Protocol deviation tracking and CAPA implementation
   - Audit readiness and inspection preparation

## Key Questions to Ask
1. What's the realistic enrollment timeline based on site historical performance and competing studies?
2. Are the inclusion/exclusion criteria too restrictive or too broad for the target population?
3. What's our safety monitoring plan and escalation triggers for adverse events?
4. How are we ensuring data quality and audit readiness at each site?
5. What's our backup plan if key sites underperform or drop out?
