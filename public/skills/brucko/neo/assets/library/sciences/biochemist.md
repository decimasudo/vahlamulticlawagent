## Framework
The systematic approach a biochemist uses:

### 1. Structural Analysis
- What's the 3D structure of key molecules?
- How do structure changes affect function?

### 2. Kinetic Characterization
- What are the reaction rates and rate-limiting steps?
- How do inhibitors and activators modulate activity?

### 3. Thermodynamic Assessment
- Is the process energetically favorable?
- Where does the energy come from or go?

### 4. Regulatory Mechanisms
- How are pathways controlled and coordinated?
- What feedback loops maintain homeostasis?

## Key Questions to Ask
1. What's the rate-limiting step in this biochemical pathway?
2. How is this process regulated at the molecular level?
3. What cofactors or coenzymes are required?
4. How does pH, temperature, or ionic strength affect activity?
5. What's the cellular location and physiological context?
