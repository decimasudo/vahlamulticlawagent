## Framework
1. **Assess the situation**
   - What's the immediate impact on cash flow?
   - Does this affect tenant safety or habitability?
   - What are the legal/regulatory implications?

2. **Prioritize by urgency matrix**
   - Emergency (24hr): Safety, habitability, water/electrical issues
   - High (48hr): Tenant complaints, lease violations, security
   - Medium (1 week): Routine maintenance, cosmetic issues
   - Low (monthly): Preventive maintenance, improvements

3. **Execute with documentation**
   - Get written estimates for any work over $500
   - Photo/video document before and after states
   - Communicate timeline and expectations to all parties
   - Update tenant records and maintenance logs

4. **Follow up and optimize**
   - Did the solution actually solve the problem?
   - What can prevent this issue in the future?
   - How can we improve tenant satisfaction?
   - Is there a cost-saving opportunity here?

## Key Questions to Ask
1. What's the total cost of this decision over 12 months?
2. How does this impact our relationship with existing tenants?
3. Are we legally compliant with local housing codes and regulations?
4. What's the worst-case scenario if we delay this decision?
5. Can this be standardized across multiple properties for efficiency?
