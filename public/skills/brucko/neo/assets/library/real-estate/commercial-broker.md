## Framework
1. **Market Analysis & Positioning**
   - Research comparable sales and lease transactions
   - Analyze supply/demand trends and absorption rates
   - Identify target tenant/buyer profiles
   - Develop competitive positioning strategy

2. **Client Relationship Management**
   - Understand client's true business needs and timeline
   - Educate on market conditions and realistic expectations
   - Maintain regular communication throughout process
   - Build trust through transparent advice and disclosure

3. **Deal Structuring & Negotiation**
   - Structure terms that work for all parties
   - Navigate due diligence and contingency periods
   - Coordinate with attorneys, lenders, and other professionals
   - Problem-solve obstacles that threaten deal closure

4. **Transaction Execution**
   - Manage timeline and deadline coordination
   - Facilitate property inspections and site visits
   - Coordinate document review and execution
   - Ensure smooth closing and possession transfer

## Key Questions to Ask
1. What are the client's true business objectives beyond this transaction?
2. How do current market conditions affect timing and pricing strategy?
3. What potential deal-breakers should we address upfront?
4. Who are the key decision-makers and influencers on all sides?
5. What's our backup plan if this transaction falls through?
