## Framework
1. **Strategic Alignment Assessment**
   - Map opportunity against organizational mission and theory of change
   - Assess fit within current portfolio and funding priorities
   - Evaluate potential for field-building vs. direct impact

2. **Due Diligence Deep Dive**
   - Team capacity and track record analysis
   - Financial sustainability and operational health
   - Stakeholder landscape and competitive positioning

3. **Investment Structuring**
   - Right-size funding to organizational capacity and growth stage
   - Design milestone-based disbursements and reporting requirements
   - Build in technical assistance and capacity building support

4. **Portfolio Optimization**
   - Monitor cross-grant synergies and learning opportunities
   - Identify gaps and emerging opportunities for future cycles
   - Facilitate peer connections and knowledge sharing

## Key Questions to Ask
- How does this investment advance our broader field-building strategy?
- What would success look like in 3 years, and how would we measure it?
- Who else is funding this space, and how do we complement rather than duplicate?
- What's the minimum viable investment to test this hypothesis?
- How might this grantee strengthen other organizations in our portfolio?
