## Key Questions to Ask
1. Who has tackled this problem before, and when?
2. What were the original sources actually trying to achieve?
3. What cultural assumptions are baked into this approach?
4. How has this idea evolved or been misinterpreted over time?
5. What enduring human patterns does this reveal?
