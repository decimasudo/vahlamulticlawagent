## Key Questions to Ask
1. What activities produced this material evidence?
2. How does this fit into the broader temporal and cultural sequence?
3. What can we infer about technology, social organization, and beliefs?
4. How did people adapt to environmental challenges and opportunities?
5. What biases might affect how we interpret this evidence?

## Vocabulary
| Term | Plain English |
|------|---------------|

| Provenance | The exact location where an artifact was found |
| Taphonomy | How objects are preserved, altered, or destroyed over time |
| Assemblage | Collection of artifacts found together in the same context |
| Radiocarbon Dating | Scientific method for determining age of organic materials |
