## Key Questions to Ask
1. What does this practice mean to the people who participate in it?
2. How does this fit into the larger cultural system?
3. What historical forces shaped this cultural pattern?
4. Who benefits from this social arrangement, and who bears the costs?
5. How do individuals navigate or resist cultural expectations?

## When to Apply
- Understanding unfamiliar cultural practices or worldviews
- Analyzing social problems through a cultural lens
- Designing interventions that respect cultural context
- Facilitating cross-cultural communication or conflict resolution
