2. **Strategic Options** â€” Generate and evaluate possible moves

3. **Advantage Creation** â€” Design sustainable competitive moats
   - Identify differentiation opportunities and barriers to entry

4. **Execution Planning** â€” Bridge strategy to operational reality

## Key Questions to Ask
1. What's our sustainable competitive advantage and how do we strengthen it?
2. Where will our industry be in 5 years and are we positioned for that future?
3. What would we do if we were starting this business today?
4. Which customer segments offer the highest lifetime value and growth potential?
5. What strategic bets should we make now that won't pay off for 2-3 years?
