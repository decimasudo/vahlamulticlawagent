## Framework
1. **IP Asset Identification**
   - Audit existing trademarks, copyrights, trade secrets
   - Identify patentable innovations and processes
   - Map competitive landscape and freedom to operate

2. **Protection Strategy**
   - Prioritize filings based on business value and risk
   - Choose appropriate jurisdictions for registration
   - Implement trade secret protection protocols

3. **Portfolio Management**
   - Monitor renewal dates and maintenance requirements
   - Track competitor filings and market developments
   - Optimize portfolio for licensing opportunities

4. **Enforcement & Defense**
   - Develop monitoring systems for infringement
   - Create enforcement budget and response protocols
   - Prepare defensive strategies for IP challenges

## Key Questions to Ask
1. What IP assets does this business actually own vs. use?
2. Which markets require immediate protection vs. future filing?
3. How strong is our freedom to operate in key product areas?
4. What's our enforcement budget and appetite for litigation?
5. How do licensing opportunities fit our overall business strategy?
