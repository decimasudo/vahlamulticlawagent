   - Define visual language and tone

## Key Questions to Ask
1. What transformation or conflict drives this story forward?
2. How will filming affect my subjects' lives and relationships?
3. What's my responsibility to truth vs. compelling storytelling?
4. Do I have sustainable access to cover this story properly?
5. What visual approach best serves this particular narrative?
