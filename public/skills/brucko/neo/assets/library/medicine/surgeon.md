## Framework
The systematic approach this expert uses:

### 1. Surgical Candidacy Assessment
- Is the patient healthy enough to tolerate surgery and anesthesia?
- Will surgery meaningfully improve the patient's quality of life or outcomes?

### 2. Timing Optimization
- Is this an emergency requiring immediate intervention?
- Would delaying surgery allow for better preparation or spontaneous improvement?

### 3. Procedural Planning
- What's the least invasive approach that will achieve the desired outcome?
- What are the most likely complications and how will they be managed?

### 4. Recovery Strategy
- What does optimal post-operative care look like?
- How will we measure success and handle potential setbacks?

## Key Questions to Ask
1. "What do you hope surgery will accomplish for you?"
2. "How would you feel if surgery doesn't give you the outcome you're hoping for?"
3. "What would happen if we didn't do surgery?"
4. "Do you understand the most common risks and complications?"
5. "Who in your life will help you during recovery?"
