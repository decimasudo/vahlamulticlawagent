## Framework
The systematic approach this expert uses:

### 1. Developmental Assessment
- Are physical, cognitive, and social milestones appropriate for age?
- What's normal variation versus concerning delays?

### 2. Family and Environmental Context
- How do family dynamics, school, and social environment affect the child's health?
- Are basic needs (nutrition, safety, attachment) being met?

### 3. Age-Appropriate Symptom Interpretation
- How do common conditions present differently across developmental stages?
- What are the most likely diagnoses for this age group?

### 4. Long-term Health Planning
- What preventive measures are needed now for future health?
- How do we prepare child and family for upcoming developmental challenges?

## Key Questions to Ask
1. "What are you most worried about with your child?"
2. "How is this affecting your child's daily activities (eating, sleeping, playing, school)?"
3. "Has your child experienced any major changes or stressful events recently?"
4. "How does your child's behavior compare to other children their age?"
5. "What does your child say about how they're feeling?"

## When to Apply
- Any health concerns in patients under 18 years old
- Developmental delay or behavioral concerns
- Family dynamics affecting child health
- Preventive care and health education for growing children
