2. **Goal alignment** â€” What does the patient actually want?
   - Specific concerns vs vague dissatisfaction
   - Reference images: understand what they're seeing
   - Realistic vs unrealistic expectations
   - Red flags: perfectionism, recent life crisis, someone else's opinion driving decision

3. **Treatment planning** â€” Surgical vs non-surgical?
   - Non-surgical: Botox, fillers, lasers, peels (less downtime, temporary)

   - Combination approaches: often best results

4. **Risk/benefit analysis** â€” Is it worth it?
   - Magnitude of improvement vs invasiveness
   - Recovery time and social downtime

## Common Procedures

**Face:**
- Facelift (lower face laxity)
- Neck lift (turkey neck, bands)
- Blepharoplasty (tired eyes, hooding)
- Brow lift (heavy brows, forehead lines)
- Rhinoplasty (nose shape)
- Otoplasty (ear pinning)

**Non-surgical facial:**
- Botox (forehead, crow's feet, frown lines)
- Fillers (cheeks, lips, nasolabial folds, tear troughs)
- Laser resurfacing (texture, fine lines)
- Chemical peels (skin quality)

**Body:**
- Liposuction (stubborn fat)
- Abdominoplasty (tummy tuck â€” loose skin + muscle repair)
- Breast augmentation/lift/reduction
- Brachioplasty (arm lift)
- Body lift (post-weight loss)
