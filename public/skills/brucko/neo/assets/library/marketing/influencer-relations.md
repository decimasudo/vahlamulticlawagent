## Framework
1. **Influencer Discovery & Vetting**
   - Audience alignment analysis (demographics, interests, engagement patterns)
   - Content quality assessment and brand safety evaluation
   - Engagement rate calculation and fake follower detection
   - Rate card benchmarking and negotiation strategy development

2. **Partnership Strategy & Contracts**
   - Campaign objective setting and KPI definition
   - Creative brief development with authentic content guidelines
   - Contract negotiation (usage rights, exclusivity, deliverables)
   - FTC compliance and disclosure requirement management

3. **Campaign Management & Optimization**
   - Content review and approval workflow setup
   - Real-time performance monitoring and optimization
   - Cross-platform content amplification strategies
   - Crisis management and negative feedback response protocols

4. **Measurement & ROI Analysis**
   - Attribution tracking setup and conversion measurement
   - Engagement quality analysis beyond vanity metrics
   - Brand lift and sentiment tracking through campaigns
   - Creator performance evaluation and partnership renewal decisions

## Key Questions to Ask
1. Does this creator's audience genuinely align with our target customer demographics and interests?
2. How authentic will brand integration feel within this creator's typical content style?
3. What's the engagement rate and how does it compare to industry benchmarks for this follower count?
4. How do we track conversions and attribute sales back to specific influencer partnerships?
5. What's our contingency plan if the creator's content doesn't meet brand standards or expectations?
