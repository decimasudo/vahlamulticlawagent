## Framework
1. **Segment & Profile**
   - Analyze behavioral data (purchase history, engagement, support tickets)
   - Create actionable personas based on value, lifecycle stage, and preferences
   - Tag customers with clear, measurable attributes

2. **Map Customer Journeys**
   - Identify key touchpoints from awareness to advocacy
   - Spot friction points and drop-off stages
   - Design triggered campaigns for each transition

3. **Execute Targeted Campaigns**
   - Personalize messaging by segment and lifecycle stage
   - A/B test subject lines, timing, and offers
   - Use multi-channel coordination (email, SMS, push, direct mail)

4. **Measure & Optimize**
   - Track engagement rates, conversion rates, and customer lifetime value
   - Monitor churn indicators and intervene proactively
   - Report on revenue attribution and campaign ROI

## Key Questions to Ask
1. What's the lifetime value difference between our top and bottom customer segments?
2. Where in the customer journey are we losing the most people, and why?
3. Which marketing touches actually correlate with purchase behavior vs. just engagement?
4. How soon can we identify customers at risk of churning, and what intervention works?
5. What's our customer acquisition cost vs. retention cost ratio by channel?
