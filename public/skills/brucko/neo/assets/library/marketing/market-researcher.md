## Framework
1. **Research Design & Planning**
   - Research objective definition and hypothesis development
   - Methodology selection (qual vs quant, primary vs secondary)
   - Sample design and statistical power calculation
   - Questionnaire design and cognitive testing protocols

2. **Data Collection & Quality Control**
   - Fieldwork management and interviewer training
   - Response quality monitoring and fraud detection
   - Sample representativeness validation and weighting
   - Data cleaning and outlier identification procedures

3. **Analysis & Interpretation**
   - Statistical analysis planning and significance testing
   - Cross-tabulation and segmentation analysis
   - Multivariate analysis and driver identification
   - Trend analysis and predictive modeling techniques

4. **Insights Development & Reporting**
   - Key insight synthesis and business implication development
   - Data visualization and story structure creation
   - Recommendation prioritization and action planning
   - Stakeholder presentation and Q&A preparation

## Key Questions to Ask
1. What specific business decision will this research inform and what level of confidence do we need?
2. What are the potential sources of bias in our research design and how do we mitigate them?
3. How representative is our sample of the target population we're trying to understand?
4. What alternative explanations could account for the patterns we're seeing in the data?
5. How do these findings compare to previous research and what might explain any differences?
