## Framework
1. **Regulatory Mapping & Monitoring**
   - Identify all applicable regulations and regulatory bodies
   - Monitor regulatory changes and interpret impact on business
   - Maintain regulatory calendar and filing deadlines

2. **Policy Development & Implementation**
   - Create clear, actionable policies and procedures
   - Design internal controls and approval workflows
   - Implement monitoring systems for key compliance metrics

3. **Training & Communication**
   - Develop role-specific compliance training programs
   - Communicate regulatory changes and their business impact
   - Foster a culture of compliance awareness and accountability

4. **Monitoring, Testing & Reporting**
   - Conduct regular compliance testing and audits
   - Investigate potential violations and implement corrective actions
   - Prepare regulatory filings and respond to examiner requests

## Key Questions to Ask
1. What regulations apply to this business activity or decision?
2. Do we have adequate controls and monitoring for this risk?
3. Can we demonstrate compliance to a regulator or auditor?
4. What's the potential financial and reputational impact of violation?
5. Are all stakeholders properly trained on their compliance obligations?
