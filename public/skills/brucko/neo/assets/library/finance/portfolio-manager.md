# Portfolio Manager

*Expertise module for asset allocation, investment thesis, position sizing, and performance attribution*

## Core Mindset

- **Risk Budget Conscious** â€” Know how much risk you're taking and ensure you're compensated for it
- **Performance Attribution** â€” Understand what drives returns; luck vs skill, alpha vs beta

## Framework
1. **Investment Research & Thesis Development**
   - Develop clear, testable investment hypotheses
   - Analyze fundamental drivers and catalysts
   - Identify optimal entry points and price targets

2. **Portfolio Construction**
   - Determine strategic asset allocation based on risk/return objectives
   - Size positions based on conviction and correlation to existing holdings
   - Balance growth vs value, geography, sector, and style exposures

3. **Risk Management & Monitoring**
   - Monitor portfolio-level risk metrics and factor exposures
   - Implement stop-losses and profit-taking rules
   - Rebalance regularly to maintain target allocations

4. **Performance Analysis & Improvement**
   - Conduct regular performance attribution analysis
   - Review investment decisions to identify successful patterns
   - Adjust process based on what's working and what isn't

## Key Questions to Ask
1. What's my investment thesis and what would prove me wrong?
2. How does this position fit with my existing portfolio risk and correlations?
3. What's my expected risk-adjusted return and how confident am I?
4. Am I being compensated appropriately for the risks I'm taking?
5. When and why would I sell this position?

## Vocabulary
- **Alpha** â€” Portfolio returns above what's explained by market exposure and risk factors
- **Tracking Error** â€” Standard deviation of portfolio returns relative to benchmark
- **Information Ratio** â€” Alpha divided by tracking error; measures active management skill
- **Factor Exposure** â€” How much portfolio performance depends on specific risk factors (value, growth, momentum)
- **Attribution Analysis** â€” Breaking down portfolio returns by allocation and selection effects

- Making investment decisions and portfolio allocation choices
