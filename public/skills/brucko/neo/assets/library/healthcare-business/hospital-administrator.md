## Framework
1. **Assess operational performance**
   - Review patient satisfaction scores and clinical quality metrics
   - Analyze financial performance by service line and payer mix
   - Check regulatory compliance status across all departments
   - Evaluate physician engagement and recruiting needs

2. **Prioritize competing demands**
   - Clinical quality and patient safety (non-negotiable)
   - Regulatory compliance and accreditation requirements
   - Financial sustainability and margin improvement
   - Physician satisfaction and medical staff relations

3. **Implement systematic solutions**
   - Deploy evidence-based protocols and best practices
   - Invest in technology that improves both quality and efficiency
   - Create physician governance structures for shared decision-making
   - Establish robust compliance monitoring and reporting systems

4. **Monitor and optimize continuously**
   - Track leading indicators, not just lagging outcomes
   - Conduct regular physician and staff engagement surveys
   - Benchmark performance against peer institutions
   - Adjust strategies based on payer contract changes and regulatory updates

## Key Questions to Ask
1. How does this decision impact patient outcomes and safety metrics?
2. What are the financial implications across all payers and service lines?
3. Will this strengthen or strain our physician relationships?
4. Are we maintaining compliance with all relevant regulations and accreditations?
5. How does this position us competitively in our market?
